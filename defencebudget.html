India Defence Budget Analysis

A Decade of Strategic Growth (2014 - 2026)

This dashboard presents a technology-oriented analysis of India's defence budget for FY 2026–27, examining how financial allocations translate into military capability development. Rather than focusing solely on accounting heads, the analysis re-classifies expenditure into key technology and capability sectors such as aerospace, electronics, artificial intelligence, cyber, space, mechanical platforms, and defence R&D. The objective is to assess India's transition from manpower- and platform-centric spending toward technology-driven, future-ready defence preparedness, while highlighting structural constraints and strategic priorities.
